How to fry a egg

Things you need

cooking oil

egg

small frying pan

How to cook

step 1: turn on the stove

step 2: put the frying pan on the stove

step 3: pour some cooking oil into the pan

step 4: crack the egg open

step 5: pour the egg into the pan WITHOUT the eggshell

step 6: flip the egg until the egg turns solid

step 7: put it on a plate

step 8: Eat the egg!!!
